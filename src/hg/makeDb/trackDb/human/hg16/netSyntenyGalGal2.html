Description

This track shows the $o_organism/$organism chains for syntenic regions of $o_organism ($o_date - $o_db) and $organism.

Methods

The $o_Organism/$Organism Alignment Net track (netGalGal2) was processed with netFilter -syn to produce this set of syntenic alignments.
