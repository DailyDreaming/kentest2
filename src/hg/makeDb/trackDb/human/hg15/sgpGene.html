Description

This track shows gene predictions from the SGP program, which is being developed at the Grup de Recerca en Informàtica Biomèdica (GRIB) at Institut Municipal d'Investigació Mèdica (IMIM) in Barcelona. To predict genes in a genomic query, SGP combines geneid predictions with tblastx comparisons of the genomic query against other genomic sequences. In this particular annotation, the Feb. 2003 (mm3) assembly of the mouse genome was used to find homology evidence between the two genomes.
