Description

The RfamFull track shows ncRNAs from the RNA families database at the Wellcome Trust Sanger Institute. ncRNAs are represented by thick lines.

Methods

Human entries from Rfam were extracted and aligned against the human genome using blat. The Rfam database is described in Griffiths-Jones et al. (2003) below.
