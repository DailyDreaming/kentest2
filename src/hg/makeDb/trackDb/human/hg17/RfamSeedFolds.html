Description

The RfamSeed Folds track shows the secondary structure annotation of ncRNAs from the RNA families database at the Wellcome Trust Sanger Institute.

Display Conventions and Configuration

ncRNAs are represented by thick lines in the graphical display.

Methods

Human entries from Rfam were extracted and aligned against the human genome using blat. The Rfam database is described in Griffiths-Jones, S. et al. (2003) below.

Credits
