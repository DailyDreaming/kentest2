Description

This track displays the genes in the loci that are or have been involved in microattribution reviews. A microattribution review is a review article on the variation found within a locus or a group of loci. To participate in the review article use the wiki to submit SNPs in or near a gene under review. You are then eligible to be a co-author on the review and will get credit for the SNPs you submit (the microattribution).

Display

The status of the review is color coded in this track. Black boxes mean the review is complete, green is for active reviews, and blue is for future reviews.
