Description

This track shows the location of non-protein-coding RNA genes and pseudogenes.

Feature types include:

  • tRNA: Transfer RNA
  • rRNA: Ribosomal RNA
  • scRNA: Small cytoplasmic RNA
  • snRNA: Small nuclear RNA
  • snoRNA: Small nucleolar RNA
  • miRNA: MicroRNA
  • misc_RNA: Miscellaneous other RNA, such as RNaseP_nuc
  • Intron: Group II self-splicing intron (probable contamination)
  • UTR_element: cis-regulatory elements in UTRs of protein coding genes

Methods

tRNAscan-SE:
tRNAscan-SE 1.23. Score field contains tRNAscan-SE bit score; >20 is good, >50 is great.

Rfam5.0:
Rfam 5.0 covariance models and the INFERNAL 0.55 software using Rfam family specific cutoffs. The score field contains the INFERNAL bit score.

miRNA_Registry:
WU-BLASTN matches to human, mouse and rat miRNAs from the miRNA Registry 3.0. Candidate chicken miRNAs match a precursor miRNA with e-value < 1e-4, form a predicted hairpin structure with free energy of folding < -20 kJ.mol-1, and match the mature ~22 nt miRNA with 2 or fewer mismatches.
