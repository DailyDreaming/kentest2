Description

This track shows the human/$organism chains for syntenic regions of the human assembly (July 2003 - hg16) and the $organism assembly.

Methods

The Human/$Organism Alignment Net track (netHg16) was processed with netFilter -syn to produce this set of syntenic alignments.
