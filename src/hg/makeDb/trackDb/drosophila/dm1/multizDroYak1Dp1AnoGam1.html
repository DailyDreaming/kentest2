Description

This track displays multiz alignments of three Drosophila species and the mosquito:

  • $Organism ($date, $db)
  • D. yakuba (Apr. 2004, droYak1)
  • D. pseudoobscura (Aug. 2003, dp1)
  • A. gambiae (Sep. 2003, anoGam1)

Methods

Blastz was used to create pairwise alignments to $Organism of D. yakuba, D. pseudoobscura and A. gambiae. Alignments were chained (axtChain) and netted (chainNet, netSyntenic, netClass). Then single-coverage/best-in-genome sets of pairwise alignments were extracted from the nets. These pairwise alignments were processed into multiple alignments by multiz.
