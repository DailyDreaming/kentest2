Description

The Structural variations track shows duplications (DUP), deletions (DEL), insertions (INS) and inversions (INV) which were identified from 27 domestic dogs of 19 breeds.

Methods

The structural variations (SV) were called from 10x sequencing data. 10x reads were aligned to the CanFam4 reference by long ranger. Multiple algorithms were used to scan the SVs in different types and sizes. Individual SVs were QC filtered and merged as a nonredundant dataset.
