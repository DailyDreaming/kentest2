Description

This track shows alignments of Ebola/Zaire Genbank sequences.

Display Conventions and Configuration

Genomic locations of Genbank matches are labelled with the accession number. Click on them and use the "Outlink" to get to the Genbank record.

Methods

Genbank sequences were aligned with BLAT. Only alignments with a minimum identity of 90% that span at least 90% of the query sequence were kept.
