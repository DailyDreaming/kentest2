Description

This track shows the location of the sequences used for the selection of probes on the Affymetrix MOE430 version 2 chip set.

Methods

The sequences were mapped to the genome with blat followed by pslReps using the parameters -minCover=0.3, -minAli=0.95 and -nearTop=0.005.

Credits

Probe sequences were obtained from the Affymetrix support materials system.
