Description

The WormBase Genes track shows Sanger Gene predictions from the Wormbase v. WS120 files downloaded from the Sanger Institute FTP site. This track shows the subset of data annotated with "curated", "DNA", or "RNA" followed by one of these strings: "intron", "exon", "cds", "sequence", or "transcri".
