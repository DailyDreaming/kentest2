Description

The WormBase Genefinder track shows Sanger Gene predictions from the Wormbase v. WS140 files downloaded from the Sanger Institute FTP site. This track shows the subset of data annotated with the string "Genefinder" and found by the gene prediction program, Genefinder.

Credits
