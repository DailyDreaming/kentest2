Description

This track shows the draft assembly of the $organism genome. The genome has been sequenced to 22X coverage. See also: ABLE03 WGS information.

In dense mode, this track depicts the contigs that make up the currently viewed contig.

All components within this track are of fragment type "W": Whole Genome Shotgun contig.

Assembly details

This genome has been sequenced to 22X coverage. There are 3,670 supercontigs comprising 145,442,736 nucleotides. The largest supercontig, Cjap_Contig0 is 997038 bases. One half of the sequence, 81,667,507 bases, is contained in 445 supercontigs, all of at least 90,117 bases (N50).
