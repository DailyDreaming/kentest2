Description

This column shows the Ensembl transcript ID associated with the gene.

Ensembl is an automatic gene prediction pipeline and a major genome database and web site run by the Sanger Institute and the European Bioinformatics Institute (EMBL-EBI). It is especially effective at mapping genes that are known in one organism to another organism. Compared to other gene predictions, those of Ensembl tend to have high specificity but low sensitivity to genes not already associated with characterized mRNA or protein sequence.

Action

Click on an Ensembl ID to display the Ensembl GeneView page for the gene.
