Description

This shows the maximum absolute expression level for any tissue. Most of the values fall in the zero to 30,000 range, but a few outliers may range as high as 52,000. A value of less than 20 indicates the expression could not be detected in any tissue at levels significantly above the cross-hybridization controls.
